Featured Models

✅ Miura-Ori

Explore the efficiency of fold-and-deploy structures. Miura-Ori enables compact stowage and rapid deployment in applications like solar panel arrays for spacecraft and emergency shelters.

✅ Yoshimura Pattern

Discover the curved buckling pattern enhancing cylindrical shells. Used to improve flexibility and load distribution in deployable architectural canopies, collapsible packaging, and aerospace fuselage components.

✅ Kresling Pattern

Visualize the helical fold used in actuators and soft robotics. Kresling designs offer tunable stiffness and energy absorption for adaptive cushioning, wearable exoskeleton joints, and vibration damping systems.
