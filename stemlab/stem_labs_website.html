Welcome to STEM Labs 👋

At STEM Labs, we're on a mission to transform how students engage with STEM. By combining the thrill of race-car design with core principles of physics, engineering, and coding, we empower learners to explore their creativity, build critical thinking skills, and spark their passion for discovery. Whether you're just starting out or ready for complex builds, there's a track waiting for you.

Normal 🛠️

  • Learn basic physics: wheels, motion, and balance
  • Hands-on building with simple, intuitive parts
  • Safe and fun for all ages
  • No prior experience required
  • Build your first working race car!

Different 🧩

  • Add creative mods to your car’s design
  • Explore sensors, drag, and software logic
  • Think outside the box with problem-solving activities
  • Combine mechanics with electronics
  • Great for intermediate builders and tinkerers

Hard 💥

  • Dive into aerodynamics and propulsion systems
  • Integrate advanced coding and real-time response
  • Apply real engineering practices and analysis
  • Compete in simulated track tests and challenges
  • Built for future engineers, physicists, and innovators
