Unlocking the Power of Origami

FoldEDU is a youth-led nonprofit dedicated to unlocking the power of origami as a gateway to innovation in engineering, design, and education.

Explore Our Workshops

Our Mission

We inspire students to explore the intersection of art and science through hands-on workshops, research projects, and community outreach—turning simple folds into powerful ideas that shape the future.

Hands-On Workshops

Intro to Engineering Folds

Learn foundational origami techniques and how they apply to mechanical design.

Design Thinking Sessions

Collaborate on creating origami-inspired prototypes for real-world problems.

Advanced Fold Patterns

Dive into complex folding algorithms and their applications in robotics and aerospace.

Research Projects

Foldable Solar Panels

Investigating origami-inspired folding schemes to maximize solar array compactness and efficiency.

Origami Robotics

Developing lightweight, foldable robotic arms for delicate tasks in constrained spaces.

Community Outreach

Partnering with local schools, libraries, and community centers to bring origami-based STEM activities to underrepresented students.
