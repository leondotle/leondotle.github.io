A classic crane fold to foster focus and mindfulness.

  1. Start with a square sheet of paper.
  2. Fold diagonally both ways and unfold.
  3. Create a square base and form bird base.
  4. Fold wings, head, and tail to complete the crane.

Particularly helpful for:

  • Anxiety reduction through rhythmic folding
  • Improved fine motor control
  • Encouraging deep breathing with each step

Fold a simple origami star to train sustained attention.

  1. Cut five identical strips of paper.
  2. Fold each into a pentagon-shaped loop.
  3. Interlock loops one by one to form a star.
  4. Flatten and adjust points for symmetry.

Particularly helpful for:

  • Boosting executive function in ADHD
  • Practicing sequential planning
  • Visual-spatial skill development

üçÇ Zen Leaf

A soothing leaf model to support sensory regulation.

  1. Begin with a rectangle of paper.
  2. Fold in half lengthwise, then unfold.
  3. Create accordion folds across width.
  4. Shape the top into a gentle curve for leaf tip.

Particularly helpful for:

  • Providing tactile feedback for autism
  • Enhancing patience and persistence
  • Encouraging rhythmic hand motions
